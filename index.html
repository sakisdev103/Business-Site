Responsive Web Design

At Falcon, we are in favor of and recommend the creation of websites with responsive web design. Responsive web design is the configuration and adaptation of the website to the type of screen in real-time. Specifically, it involves the adjustment of fonts, images, backgrounds, and even the layout of the page! With the rapid increase in the number of portable electronic devices (smartphones, tablets) used to access the internet, it is observed that the visit rate of a website is 60% from portable electronic devices. Therefore, at Falcon, we believe that this service should be present on any site.
